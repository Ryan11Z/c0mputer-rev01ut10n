Reflection

During the beginning of the project I was away on exchange, so I was a little worried about if I was too far behind on the project work. However, once I got back I already had a good idea about my plan for the project, so I was able to get into the process pretty quickly. During the research stage of the project, I did have some trouble finding reliable sources, but I eventually found enough to get the information necessary for the project.

In terms of research skills, I found note-taking to be the most helpful. I felt that personally that it enabled me to be able to keep my focus on decoding the information. I would then put the information in a table, as this would then make the information easier to read and then put the facts and data into full sentences.

I enjoyed being able to put my project together on a website, as I felt that I could create a clean product that would be interesting to read. I also found that I learnt a lot of skills that I'd use in the future in terms of editing and proofreading my work.

In general, my classwork was fairly productive, and I was able to stay where I needed to be along the project. However, I often found that my productivity suffered when others were talking, but I was able to counter that by learning to shut that out of my brain, while I also found it was helpful to still work near others so that we could bounce ideas for our projects.
